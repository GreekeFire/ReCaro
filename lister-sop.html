STEP 01
Open Your Queue
Your queue shows everything assigned to you. Start here every day.

Open your personal queue link (the one you were given when you joined). Your queue page only shows tasks assigned to you — no one else's.

📷
images/lister-image1.png
Screenshot of the queue page showing a list of pending tasks

Each task in the queue shows the product title, sell price, source cost, and margin at a glance. Click any task to expand it and see everything you need to post the listing.

Sell Price
The price you will list on Carousell. Do not change it.
Product Title
Copy button included — copies the full title to your clipboard.
Description
Copy button included — copies the full description to your clipboard.
Drive Folder
Link to the product images. Click to open.
Shopee Link
The source product on Shopee. For reference only — do not use for anything else.
↻
Refreshing the queue Use the Refresh button at the top of the queue page to reload your task list. Your tasks are prepared the day before — your queue for the day is ready when you start your shift.
STEP 02
Download the Images
All images for the listing are in the Drive folder.
1

Click the Drive Folder link in the expanded task to open it.

2

Check that there are at least 5 images in the folder.

If there are fewer than 5, flag this submission before doing anything else. See the Flagging section.

3

Download all images. Select all files, right-click, and choose Download.

📷
images/lister-image2.png
Screenshot of a Google Drive folder open with product images inside
⚠
Check the cover image The first image in the folder is the cover image. Before downloading, check that it has no watermarks, brand logos, or promotional banners. If it does, flag the submission. See the Flagging section.
STEP 03
Start a New Listing on Carousell
Log in to the Carousell account and create a new listing.
1

Open Carousell and log in with the credentials you were given via Telegram.

2

Tap or click the Sell button (the + icon) to start a new listing.

📷
images/lister-image3.png
Screenshot showing the Sell / + button on Carousell
✗
Credentials are confidential Never write the Carousell login details anywhere — not in a doc, not in a message, not in your notes. If you need them resent, message your manager on Telegram.
STEP 04
Upload the Images
Upload in the correct order. The first image becomes the cover.

On the new listing screen, you will see a photo upload area. Upload the images in this order:

1st — Cover
Clean cover photo — the main product shot with a plain background. This is the most important image. Upload it first.
2nd–4th — Details
Product detail shots — angles, close-ups, what's included.
Next — Dimensions
Any images showing size, measurements, or specs.
Last — Variants
Any images showing colour or size options.
📷
images/lister-image4.png
Screenshot of the Carousell photo upload screen with images being added
ℹ
Reordering photos On Carousell you can drag photos to reorder them after uploading. The leftmost photo is the cover. Make sure the clean cover image is always in the first position.
STEP 05
Select the Category
Carousell will suggest one automatically. Pick the most specific option.

After uploading images, Carousell will automatically suggest a category based on what it detects in the photos. In most cases this will be correct.

1

Check the suggested category. If it looks correct for the product, keep it.

2

If the category is wrong or too broad, tap it and select the most specific subcategory available.

Example "Home & Living" is too broad. Pick "Home Organisation & Storage" or whichever subcategory best fits the product.

STEP 06
Fill in the Listing Details
Copy the title and description directly from your queue page.
📷
images/lister-image6.png
Screenshot of the Carousell item details form showing title, brand, and description fields
Condition
Always select Brand New. Every product comes from Shopee and has never been used.
Listing Title
Go to your queue page, open the task, and use the Copy button next to the title. Paste it here exactly as copied.
Do not retype it — copy and paste only.
Brand
Select Others / Unbranded. Never select a specific brand name.
Description
Go to your queue page, open the task, and use the Copy button next to the description. Paste it here exactly as copied.
Read the description quickly before pasting. If it mentions Shopee, Lazada, Amazon, or a brand name — fix it before pasting. See the Flagging section.
More than one
Tick the box that says "I have more than one of the same item". Always tick this.
✗
Do not edit the title or description Copy and paste exactly as given in the queue. The only exception is if you spot a brand name or competitor platform mentioned — in that case, remove it, then post. Flag the issue separately.
STEP 07
Set Delivery
Delivery only. No meetups. Custom 3–5 days. Free.
1

Under Deal methods, toggle Meet-up OFF and Delivery ON.

2

Tap Edit delivery methods.

3

Toggle Carousell Official Delivery OFF.

Leave it off — we do not use Carousell's courier service.

4

Under Arrange your own delivery, toggle Custom (3–5 days) ON.

5

Set the delivery fee to Free.

6

Tap Done.

📷
images/lister-image7.png

Correct delivery settings
  • Meet-up — OFF
  • Delivery — ON
  • Carousell Official Delivery — OFF
  • Custom (3–5 days) — ON
  • Delivery fee — Free
Do not do these
  • Enable Meet-up
  • Enable Carousell Official Delivery
  • Set any delivery fee
  • Leave delivery toggled OFF
STEP 08
Enable the Buy Button
Always enable this. It adds Buyer Protection to your listing.

The Buy Button is Carousell's built-in payment feature. Always enable it. Here is why it matters:

More sales
Some buyers will only pay through Carousell's platform — they won't pay outside it. Enabling this captures those buyers.
Buyer Protection
When enabled, your listing shows a Buyer Protection banner. This builds trust and makes buyers more likely to purchase.
1

Find the List with 'Buy' button section.

2

Select Enable 'Buy' button. Never select Disable.

📷

STEP 09
Set the Price
Use the sell price from your queue. Do not change it.
1

In the Price field, enter the Sell Price shown in your queue for this task.

The sell price is pre-calculated. Do not round it, do not adjust it.

2

Make sure Fixed Price is toggled ON so buyers cannot make offers below the listed price.

3

If you see an Add WhatsApp button option and it is available for this listing — enable it.

This only appears for eligible categories. If it does not appear, skip this step.

STEP 10
Post the Listing
List it, then copy the Carousell URL immediately.
1

Tap or click List Now to post the listing.

2

Wait for the listing to go live. Carousell will show you the posted listing.

3

Copy the full URL of the live listing from your browser's address bar.

It must start with https://www.carousell.sg/p/ — copy the whole thing.

Copy the URL before moving on You need this URL to mark the task as done. If you forget to copy it, go to your Carousell profile, find the listing, and copy it from there.
STEP 11
Mark as Done in Your Queue
Submit the Carousell URL to close out the task.
1

Go back to your queue page and find the task you just posted.

2

Click the task to expand it. Select the Mark Done tab.

3

Paste the Carousell URL into the Carousell Listing URL field.

Must start with https://www.carousell.sg/p/ — the queue will reject it if not.

4

Click Submit Listing. The task will move to Done and disappear from your active queue.

⚑ FLAG
Flagging a Bad Submission
Flag, fix it yourself, then post. Never skip a task.

If you open a task and spot a problem with the researcher's submission, you must flag it. Flagging logs the issue against the researcher's record and notifies the manager. After flagging you still fix and post the listing yourself.

// When to flag
⚑
Fewer than 5 images in the Drive folder
Flag before doing anything else. You will not have enough images to post a proper listing.
⚑
Cover image has a watermark, brand logo, or promotional banner
The cover image should be a clean product shot. Branding or watermarks on the cover will hurt the listing.
⚑
Description mentions Shopee, Lazada, Amazon, or any other platform
Remove the mention before posting. Flag so the researcher is corrected.
⚑
Description or title contains a brand name
Replace the brand name with a generic product name (e.g. "Ikea storage box" → "stackable storage box"). Flag so it is logged.
// How to flag
1

Open the task in your queue and click the Flag Issue tab.

2

Write a clear description of the problem in the reason field.

Good flag reasons "Only 3 images in Drive folder"
"Cover image has a large Shopee watermark"
"Description mentions Shopee twice — removed before posting"
"Title contains brand name 'Philips' — replaced with 'electric shaver'"
3

Click Submit Flag. The queue will switch back to the Mark Done tab automatically.

4

Fix the problem yourself, then post the listing and mark it done as normal.

Remove brand names. Remove competitor platform mentions. If the cover image has branding, use the next best image as the cover instead.

⚠
Never skip a flagged task Flagging is a note to management — it does not remove the task from your queue. You are responsible for fixing and posting every task assigned to you, even flagged ones.
